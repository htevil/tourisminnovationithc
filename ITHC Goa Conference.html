ITHC Goa Conference

Indian Tourism and Hospitality Congress is going to host INTERNATIONAL TOURISM CONFERENCE On Sustaining Tourist Destinations: Implications for Developing Countries to be organized by Indian Institute of Tourism and Travel Management during 3-5 November 2017.
Theme: “Sustaining Tourist Destinations: Implications for Developing Countries”

Sl. No. Sub-thematic/Technical Areas
1 Sustainable Tourism: A tool to address developmental challenges
2 Challenges for Tourism Sustainability in Developing Countries
3 Sustainable Development and Destination Competitiveness
4 Adventure Tourism and Water Sports: Opportunities & Challenges
5 Cases on Sustainable Tourism Development

Persons interested to participate in the conference are welcome to contribute their research papers/ research notes/ case studies/ brief research notes on the themes or related subjects with specific recommendations for discussions in technical sessions.


IMPORTANT DATES

Abstract Submission: 15 September 2017
Notification of Abstract Acceptance: 10 October 2017
Last date of registration: 15 October 2017
Date of full paper submission: 03 – 05 November 2017


Note: Abstract and full paper can be sent to email: conference.iittmgoa@gmail.com

Selected Papers presented at the Conference would also be considered for publication in the International Journal of Tourism & Travel & Tourism Innovations (UGC Approved Journals) after due diligence.

Please find attached the detailed conference announcement for your reference please. Looking forward to meet you all and wishing you a great time.

Prof. Deepak Gupta
