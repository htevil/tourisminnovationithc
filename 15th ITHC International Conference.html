15 ITHC International Conference NDIAN TOURISM AND HOSPITALITY CONGRESS (ITHC)

Theme: “Transforming Tourism and Hospitality Business: Embracing Technology and Sustainability”
Dates: 02-06 October 2024
Venue: Wolverhampton, England
Organized in association with University of Wolverhampton Business School, Wolverhampton. UK


ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

The tourism and hospitality industry is undergoing a profound transformation driven by the dual forces of technological innovation and the imperative for sustainability. This evolution is reshaping the landscape of travel, lodging, and services, necessitating a rethinking of traditional business models and practices. Embracing these changes is not merely an option but a necessity for staying competitive and relevant in a rapidly evolving market.

In an era where our world is rapidly evolving, the tourism and hospitality sectors stand at the forefront of this transformation, driven by technological advancements and the pressing need for sustainable practices. Today, we gather to explore the dynamic intersection of technology and sustainability, and how these forces are reshaping our industry. From smart hotels and AI-driven customer experiences to eco-friendly travel solutions and sustainable business models, the future of tourism and hospitality is being redefined.

This conference will serve as a platform to share insights, exchange innovative ideas, and collaborate on strategies that will not only enhance operational efficiency and customer satisfaction but also ensure that our industry contributes positively to the global sustainability agenda. As we delve into thought-provoking sessions, panel discussions, and interactive workshops, let us seize this opportunity to learn from each other, forge new partnerships, and commit to a future where technology and sustainability are the cornerstones of our success. The purpose of this conference is to bring together research and practice.

CONTRIBUTION

Persons interested to participate in the conference are welcome to contribute their research papers/ research notes/ case studies/ brief research notes on the themes or related subjects with specific recommendations for discussions in technical sessions. Abstract of maximum 300 words in MS-word format (letter size 12 point, Times New Roman font) should reach by 25th July 2024 on the following address: info@ithc.in

Content of Papers
Papers should demonstrate critical rigour and make clear their contribution to knowledge. They should also state their implications in relation to the theme of the conference, and their contribution to one or more of research, education, policy or practice

Publication of Accepted Papers
Prior to the conference, Indian Tourism and Hospitality Congress plans to bring out special issue of International Journal ‘Tourism Innovations’ and an edited book from a reputed publisher bearing ISBN number. It is desired that research papers should be submitted for Journal and Book well in time and strictly as per the guidelines.


FORMAT FOR PRESENTATIONS

The presentation should be prepared using Microsoft PowerPoint. The presentation will then be copied onto a CD Rom and will be ready for you to present at your allotted time. Not more than 15 PowerPoint slides should be included covering background, aims, methodology and findings.
