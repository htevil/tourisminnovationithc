WHO CAN BE THE MEMBER?

  • Any teacher working in University/ college / institution imparting Tourism/ Hospitality education may become the active member / life member of the Congress.
  • Any Individual / organization related to tourism & hospitality research/academics/trade may become life member of the Congress.
  • Any individual carrying research work in Tourism/Hospitality and educational institution may become Associate life member of the Congress.
